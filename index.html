About the Academy

Naidu Cricket Academy, located in Shantinagar Bhilai, is one of the most recognized cricket training centers in the region.

The academy focuses on developing young cricketers with professional coaching, modern training methods, and high-quality practice facilities.

With experienced coaches and a large cricket ground, the academy helps players improve their batting, bowling, fielding, and match strategies. Many students get opportunities to compete at district and higher competitive levels.

The academy is known for its discipline, structured training sessions, and strong cricket culture that inspires players to pursue excellence in the sport.
